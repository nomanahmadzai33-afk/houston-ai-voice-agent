TalkServe AI concept

Tolo Kabab House AI Receptionist

A restaurant-ready phone agent that answers calls, handles table requests, captures reservation details, and escalates to staff only when needed.

Agent profile

Warm, fast, and built for busy dinner service

  • Answers every incoming call, day or night
  • Collects reservation details in under 90 seconds
  • Handles menu, hours, address, and parking questions
  • Transfers VIP, complaint, or exception cases to staff

What it does

Core call flows for a restaurant

01

Reservation intake

Captures date, time, party size, guest name, phone number, and special requests.

02

General questions

Answers location, hours, parking, wait time policy, and dining experience questions.

03

Large party routing

Flags groups, catering, and event requests for human follow-up instead of promising unavailable bookings.

04

Safe escalation

Transfers allergy, complaint, payment dispute, or urgent requests to staff with a short summary.

How it sounds

Sample opening

Agent: Thank you for calling Tolo Kabab House. This is your reservation assistant. How can I help you today?

Caller: I need a table for four tomorrow night.

Agent: I can help with that. What time would you like to come in, and may I have the name for the reservation?

Caller: 7:30 under Ahmad.

Agent: Perfect. I have a table request for four guests tomorrow at 7:30 PM under Ahmad. What is the best phone number for confirmation?

Launch path

What your friend needs to connect next

1

Pick the voice stack: `Twilio + OpenAI`, `Vapi`, or `Retell`.

2

Connect reservation storage: `Google Sheets`, `Airtable`, or the restaurant’s booking system.

3

Replace placeholder business facts with real hours, menu FAQ, booking limits, and transfer number.

4

Test missed-call recovery, noisy callers, no-availability handling, and SMS confirmations.

Business rules

Guardrails that matter in production

  • Never confirm a reservation unless the booking tool says it is available.
  • Repeat the booking summary before ending the call.
  • Do not guess about allergens, halal status, or ingredients.
  • Escalate complaints and payment issues to a human immediately.
  • Capture callback details anytime the call quality is poor.
